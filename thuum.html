Thu'um

Thu'um is a card game based on Skyrim's Shout System. The goal of the game is to collect three words indicated on the cards to form a shout. Players hurl shouts at each other to take away each other's health until one survives and emerges as the victor.

My role in this project was creating and designing all 21 cards in the game. I designed all of these cards entirely within Photoshop. I paid attention to details such as typography, spacing, and color to evoke a Skyrim-esque theme. Below is a sample of my work:
